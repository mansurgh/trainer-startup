# Privacy Policy for Trainer

**Last updated:** October 15, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Trainer ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Profile Data:** Name, age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals
- **Workout Data:** Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, workout duration
- **Nutrition Data:** Food intake, calorie tracking, meal photos
- **Photos:** Body progress photos, technique analysis photos, food photos
- **Usage Data:** App interaction, feature usage, performance metrics

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Analytics:** App crashes, errors, performance data (via Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics)
- **Location:** Approximate location (if you grant permission) for weather-based recommendations

## How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Personalization**
   - Generate customized workout plans based on your goals
   - Provide AI-powered nutrition recommendations
   - Track your progress and achievements

2. **AI Features**
   - Analyze exercise technique from photos (via OpenAI API)
   - Generate meal recommendations (via OpenAI API)
   - Create personalized training programs (via OpenAI API)

3. **App Improvement**
   - Analyze app usage to improve features
   - Debug crashes and errors
   - Optimize performance

4. **Communication**
   - Send workout reminders (if enabled)
   - Notify about achievements and milestones
   - Provide important app updates

## Data Sharing

We share your data with the following third parties:

### OpenAI (AI Features)
- **What:** Photos you upload for technique/food analysis, workout preferences
- **Why:** To provide AI-powered recommendations and analysis
- **How:** Data is sent via secure HTTPS to OpenAI API
- **Note:** You explicitly consent when using AI features

### Supabase (Backend Storage)
- **What:** All your profile, workout, and nutrition data
- **Why:** To store and sync your data across devices
- **How:** Encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Location:** EU servers (GDPR compliant)

### Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
- **What:** Anonymous usage data, crash reports
- **Why:** To improve app stability and user experience
- **How:** No personally identifiable information

### Open Food Facts
- **What:** Product barcodes you scan
- **Why:** To retrieve nutritional information
- **How:** Anonymous API calls, no personal data sent

## We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your data to third parties
- ❌ Show personalized ads based on your data
- ❌ Share your photos publicly without consent
- ❌ Use your data for purposes other than stated above

## Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

1. **Access** - Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction** - Update or correct your information
3. **Deletion** - Request deletion of your account and all data ("Right to be Forgotten")
4. **Portability** - Export your data in a machine-readable format
5. **Opt-Out** - Disable analytics and non-essential data collection
6. **Withdraw Consent** - Stop using AI features that process your photos

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@trainer.app

## Data Retention

- **Active Account:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Account:** All data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Backups:** Data in backups is deleted within 90 days
- **Analytics:** Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- ✅ **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- ✅ **Authentication:** Secure login via Supabase Auth
- ✅ **Access Control:** Role-based access to your data
- ✅ **Regular Audits:** Security reviews and updates
- ✅ **No Plain Text:** Passwords are hashed, never stored in plain text

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Trainer is not intended for users under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

## AI Data Processing Disclosure

### OpenAI Integration
When you use AI features (workout generation, technique analysis, nutrition analysis):

1. **Photos:** Sent to OpenAI API for analysis, not stored by OpenAI per their policy
2. **Text Data:** Your workout preferences, goals sent to generate recommendations
3. **Processing Location:** OpenAI servers (USA)
4. **Retention:** OpenAI retains data for 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deleted
5. **Privacy:** Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy

### Your Control
- ✅ AI features are opt-in - you choose when to use them
- ✅ You can use the app without AI features
- ✅ Delete AI-generated data anytime from your profile
